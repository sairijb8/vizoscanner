Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Vizo Scanner, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

The Short Version: Vizo Scanner is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or share your documents, camera data, or personal information on any server.

1. Information We Collect

Vizo Scanner does not require any user registration or account creation. We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Document & Camera Data

All document scanning and processing are performed locally on your device. The app requires access to your camera and storage to function, but:

  • Your camera feed is only used for scanning within the app.
  • Your documents are saved directly to your device's memory.
  • We have no access to your files or images.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party tracking or analytics services that collect your data. Since the app is free and has no ads, there are no advertising networks tracking your behavior.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored exclusively on your own device, the security of your documents depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device encryption and screen locks to protect your files.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
